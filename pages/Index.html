About Us

Kingdom Worship Ministry is a faith-based Christian organization that is part of Bride City International Church. We are called to a 24/7 worship vision beyond denominational borders, reaching every nation with praise and healing.

Founded on February 28, 2001, by Bishop Magnus Onyeaku, our ministry exists to ignite hearts in worship and to equip believers with practical discipleship in love and service.
