Church Membership

Through these classes and trainings, individuals are equipped to fully embrace their role as members of the
church, building lasting relationships, serving in various capacities, and advancing God’s kingdom.

Foundation Class

The purpose of foundation class is to establish new
believers in the foundational truths of Christianity,
building a solid spiritual base for their journey with
God. This program introduces participants to the
core teachings of the Bible, providing a clear
understanding of salvation, the nature of God, faith,
and Christian living. It helps them discover their
identity in Christ and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Maturity Class

The purpose of KWM Maturity Training is to
adequately equip believers with spiritual maturity,
leadership skills, sound moral lifestyle and a
deeper understanding of God’s word, enabling
them to effectively serve in various departments of
the church, ministries and positively impact their
communities and generation for God.

School of Ministry

KWM School of Ministry is one of the leading
Bible schools in the Nation of Nigeria where
Thousands have graduated with firebrand passion
to change their world for God and impart the globe
with the Gospel of Christ thereby covering the
earth with the Glory of God. Now, the school offers
sound spiritual, academic, as well as moral, and
character training to students from all walks of life.
