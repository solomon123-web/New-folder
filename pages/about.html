We Are Bride City I-Church

Cultivating a culture where worship is an identity, not just an event. We exist to host the presence of God.

Our Vision

We are commissioned to offer 24/7 ceaseless worship and to teach that across the Body of Christ. You can learn more about worship by connecting with us. We are non-denominational in this service.

Our Mission

To manifest the Love of God with anointed ministrations that defy denominational walls.

Our Mandate

To operate and coordinate God's 24-hour Worship across denominational walls.

Meet the Visionary

Bishop Magnus Onyeaku

Senior Pastor

Bishop Magnus Onyeaku is the Founding President of Bride City I-Church and Senior Bishop of Bride City International Church. He is an Apostle of Worship, a teacher of the Word of God and an anointed vessel of God.
